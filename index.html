What is Pop it?

Pop it literally translates to "pop it". This is a children's game, the process of which can be
compared to bursting a bubble wrap. But unlike pop-it film, you can press it with
characteristic sensations and sounds endlessly, dozens of silicone bubbles are simply
squeezed out in the other direction.

Rules of the game

Players take turns and press the bubbles. Is is imperative to finish
the row you started. You can press a maximun of 3 bubbles at the same
time (they must be nearby, you can't press several bubbles at once if they
are not right next to each other). The task is to make sure that the
opponent is the last one to leave. Whoever went last lost!!
